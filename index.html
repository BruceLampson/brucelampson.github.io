About me

Hello my name is Bruce Lampson

I am an easy going software developer with great communication skills (English and Spanish). Eager to learn and always on top of new technologies, but still able to pick the right tools for the right job. I have a vast knowledge of Agile methodologies such as Scrum. But also, I am highly committed to code quality. Therefore, I am passionate about automated builds using Jenkins/Travis and writing tests to validate my code.

I love contributing to open source. One of my favorite flavors is Zend Framework 2. You can find me on the official Zend Framework contributors page. or at the Zend yellow pages as a Zend PHP Certified Engineer.
